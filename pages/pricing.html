💰 Pricing Structure

Last Updated: 15 July 2026

At Luu Travels & Logistics, we believe in transparent and fair pricing. Below is a detailed breakdown of our pricing structure.

1. Base Fare

  • Rate per Kilometer: R2.11 per km
  • Minimum Fare: R50.00 per trip
  • Distance Calculation: Distance is calculated using the actual driving route (not straight-line distance) via OpenRouteService.
  • Fare Formula: Distance (km) × R2.11 × Number of Passengers

📏 Distance Calculation: We use real driving routes to calculate distance, ensuring accurate and fair pricing. The route is calculated using OpenRouteService, which provides actual road distance.

2. Surcharges

Service Surcharge Description
Public Holidays 15% Applied on all public holidays in South Africa
Night Travel (22:00 - 05:00) 10% Trips that start or end during night hours
Extra Luggage (per additional bag) R50.00 Beyond the standard allowance of 2 bags per passenger
Waiting Time (after 15 minutes) R2.50/min Free waiting for first 15 minutes
Child Seat Free Available upon request
Group Booking (4+ passengers) 0% No additional surcharge

3. Payment Methods

  • Card Payments: Processed securely through Paystack. Accepted cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express.
  • Cash Payments: Pay the driver directly in cash. Please have the exact amount ready.
  • EFT Payments: Available for corporate clients (contact us for details).
  • Wallet Credits: Use credits from previous refunds to pay for bookings.
  • All prices are in South African Rand (ZAR).

💳 Secure Payments: All card payments are processed through Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.

  • Promo Codes: Enter a valid promo code at checkout to receive a discount.

  • Volume Discounts: For corporate clients with frequent bookings, we offer volume discounts.

7. Price Changes

  • We reserve the right to adjust prices at any time.
  • Price changes will be communicated via email or on our website.
  • Bookings made before a price change will be honored at the original price.
  • All prices are subject to change without prior notice.
