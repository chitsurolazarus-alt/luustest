Who We Are

About Luu Travels & Logistics

Founded in 2023 and based in Windmill Park Ext 32, Boksburg, Luu Travels & Logistics (Pty) Ltd connects Gauteng and Limpopo with dependable shuttle and logistics services built on professionalism and trust.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, affordable, and professional transportation and logistics solutions that connect people and businesses efficiently while exceeding customer expectations through exceptional service delivery.

Our Vision

To become a leading transportation and logistics company in South Africa, recognized for exceptional customer service, reliability, innovation, and operational excellence.
